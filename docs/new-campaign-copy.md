# New Campaign Copy - January 2026

## Strategy Change

**Old approach:** "Worth 15 minutes to see if we're a fit?"
**Problem:** Zero value for the prospect. Why would they give you 15 minutes?

**New approach:** Lead with a tangible deliverable from the call itself.

## Key Differences from Old Copy

| Old | New |
|-----|-----|
| "How responsive is your accountant?" | "Do you know how much you're overpaying?" |
| No specific value from call | Free tax estimate ($15-40K number) |
| "Worth 15 minutes to see if we're a fit?" | "I'll run the numbers — no pitch, no cost" |
| Vague pain points | Specific dollar amounts + social proof |
| Missing Calendly in Marketing | Calendly in every email |
